A PORTRAIT IS A MOMENT IN LOVE

We say all to often that we wish our children would stay little forever. This embodies so much of our passion at Lora and Ted. As parents, we want to freeze time and remember every little moment. Our kiddos grow too fast, and while we cannot stop time, we can document it. This is what drives our passion to capture families at all their stages in life. We are blessed to be able to capture these moments of our own children and would be honored to capture yours!

LORA+TED

We are the husband and wife team behind Lora and Ted based in the Denver Area. Specializing in families, children and newborns, we love to photograph all across Denver and The Rocky Mountains. We are ready to create timeless, authentic, unique and fun photography just for you and your family. Lora+Ted are also national and local speakers, presenting at photographic workshops both online and in person. Their imagery has been featured in numerous print publications, books and blog posts. Our goal is to provide one of the best and most unique portrait experiences available in Denver and the Rocky Mountain region.

WHAT WE DO

Lora and Ted specialize in baby photography, newborn photography, pregnancy photography, child portraits and family portraits. Lora and Ted are based in Denver, Colorado and photographs clients from all areas in Denver, Littleton, Centennial, Highlands Ranch, Parker, Castle Rock, and other surrounding areas. Nationally, Lora and Ted have worked in California, Texas, Illinois, Washington, Nevada, Missouri and Kansas. We are also available for travel internationally

WHAT TO EXPECT

Portrait sessions with Lora and Ted are fun, expressive, and inspiring. We offer our clients a boutique experience and will guide you through the entire process...from your first inquiry to the delivery of your products, we are hands on and available for anything you need. We photograph at various choice outdoor locations including downtown Denver, local Denver parks and even in studio. We also frequently travel into the Mountains of Colorado photographing both local and vacationing families in such places as Keystone, Breckenridge, Winter Park, Grand Lake, Vail, Beaver Creek, Crested Butte, and Aspen, Colorado. It is not the location but how you see it that makes the shot. Our unique locations are known for capturing one of a kind images.
